We are an online shop, our headquarters are located in southern California. We are a flower store that wants to create awareness of the endangered Amur Leopard. We donate one dollar to wildlife foundations for each violet flower that we sell.

Where do Amur leopards live?

Amur Leopards live in the Amur Heilong Landscape.

How many Amur Leopards are left in the wild?

The number of Amur Leopards have increased
from 27-32 to 70-75 in the last 15 years.

How fast are Amur Leopards?

Amur Leopards can run at a speed of up to 37 miles per hour
